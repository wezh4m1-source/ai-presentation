üí°What is AI?

AI means "Artificial Intelligence". It is when computers can think and learn like people.

AI can do many things: talk to us, help us find information, play games, and solve problems.

We use AI every day. When you talk to Siri or Alexa, you use AI. When Netflix shows you movies you like, that is AI too!

üìùHow to Talk to AI: Prompts

Prompt is the instruction or text you give to an AI to tell it what you want.

A good prompt gives you a good answer. A bad prompt gives you a bad answer.

The Rule:

‚ùå Unclear Prompt ‚Üí Unclear or Bad Answer
‚úÖ Clear Prompt ‚Üí Better Answer

Example:

‚ùå Bad Prompt

Problem: Which Kurdish? Sorani or Kurmanji? The AI doesn't know!

‚úÖ Good Prompt

Clear! The AI knows exactly what to do!

üí° Remember: Be clear and specific when you talk to AI!
